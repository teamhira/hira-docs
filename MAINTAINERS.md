# Maintainers

This document defines the ownership of documentation areas across the Hira ecosystem.

## Ownership Structure
- **Architecture**: Architecture Lead
- **API (hira-core & hira-gateway)**: Backend/API Lead
- **Authentication (hira-auth)**: Security/IAM Owner
- **Infrastructure (hira-infra)**: DevOps/SRE Lead
- **Platforms**:
    - **iOS/macOS/visionOS (hira-swiftui)**: iOS Lead
    - **Android**: Android Lead
    - **Web**: Web Lead
- **Product**: Product Manager
- **QA**: Quality Lead
- **Governance & Onboarding**: Documentation Owner

## Rules
- Every significant change must be reviewed by the area owner.
- Owners are responsible for maintaining the consistency and quality of their respective areas.
