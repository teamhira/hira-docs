# iOS (SwiftUI) Overview

This document provides a concise overview of the iOS platform implementation. Detailed standards are located in the `ios/` folder.

## Multi-Platform Focus
- **SwiftUI-First**: Using the latest SwiftUI features and Concurrency patterns.
- **Multi-Platform Support**: codebase designed for future expansion to **macOS** and **visionOS**.
- **Modular Architecture**: Features are organized into discrete modules for high reuse and testing.
- **Testing & Release**: Integrated strategies for ensuring high-quality, stable releases.
