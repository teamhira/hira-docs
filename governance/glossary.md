# Glossary

## Services
- **hira-swiftui**: Native multi-platform frontend (iOS/macOS/visionOS).
- **hira-gateway**: Unified API Gateway.
- **hira-auth**: Identity service using Better Auth.
- **hira-core**: Central Go service for content and business logic.
- **hira-infra**: DevOps and IaC repository.

## Terminology
- **Mushaf**: Quran page-based layout.
- **Ayah**: A verse of the Quran.
- **Surah**: A chapter of the Quran.
- **Tafsir**: Exegesis or interpretation of the Quran.
- **Hadith**: Record of the words, actions, and silent approval of the Prophet Muhammad.
